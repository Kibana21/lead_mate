Our Vision

The Critical Challenges

Four systemic issues preventing optimal conversion performance

⏰
Lead Distribution Latency
2-4 hour average response time due to manual assignment processes. Leads go cold before agents even make contact.
🎯
Agent-Lead Mismatch
Leads not matched with right agents based on expertise, location, workload, or customer persona fit.
💬
Agent Enablement Gap
No contextual guidance on lead background, intent, or engagement strategy. Generic follow-ups that fail to resonate.
🔁
Feedback & Learning Void
No systematic feedback loop to learn from patterns. Repeated mistakes in lead handling with no improvement.

The Impact on Our Business

These challenges cost us millions in lost revenue annually. With conversion rates stagnating at 12%, we're missing 88% of opportunities. If we could improve to just 17%, that's a 40% increase in revenue from the same lead volume—with no additional marketing spend.

The Solution: AI-Orchestrated Intelligence

A comprehensive platform that combines AI, gamification, and intelligent orchestration to revolutionize the lead-to-conversion journey

⚡

Intelligent Lead Routing

Real-time AI-powered matching that assigns leads to optimal agents based on propensity scoring, expertise, location, and historical performance—within 30 seconds.

🗂️

AI-Generated Dossiers

Comprehensive lead profiles powered by Megatron with intent analysis, product recommendations, objection predictions, and compliance-aware guidance.

💬

Real-Time Coaching

In-conversation AI guidance with next-best-action recommendations, competitive intelligence, and instant compliance callouts during customer interactions.

🎮

Lead Marketplace

Gamified "Grab That Lead" experience with tiered leads (Diamond/Gold/Silver/Bronze), streak bonuses, and agent ownership driving engagement and urgency.

🗺️

Territory Gaming & Heat Maps

Live geographic visualization with proximity bonuses, surge zones, and regional competitions optimizing coverage across Singapore.

🔁

Continuous Learning

ML pipeline that learns from every interaction with two-way ratings, performance analytics, and automated optimization of routing and recommendations.

Implementation Roadmap

Phased approach with pilot validation before full deployment

Phase 1: Core Routing Engine
8 weeks | Dec 2024
Build intelligent lead routing with propensity scoring, agent matching, and basic mobile app. Foundation for all subsequent features.
Deliverables: API integration, ML models, mobile app v1, admin dashboard
Phase 2: AI-Powered Dossiers
6 weeks | Feb 2025
Integrate Megatron for comprehensive lead intelligence with product recommendations, intent analysis, and compliance awareness.
Deliverables: LLM integration, dossier generation, enhanced UI, feedback mechanism
Phase 3: Pilot Deployment
8 weeks | Apr 2025
Deploy to 200-300 agents in Central Singapore. Measure impact, gather feedback, and validate business case before scaling.
Success Target: 20% conversion improvement, <10 min response, 75% adoption
Phase 4: Gamification & Marketplace
6 weeks | Jun 2025
Launch full gamification features: marketplace, tiering, streaks, badges, squads, heat maps, and achievement system.
Deliverables: Marketplace UI, point system, leaderboards, squad features, geo-gaming
Phase 5: Full Rollout
12 weeks | Q3 2025
Deploy to all 6,000+ agents across Singapore with regional phased approach. Comprehensive training, 24/7 support, and continuous optimization.
Strategy: East → West → North rollout with dedicated change management
